Understanding Parameter Swapping

The core puzzle calls itself dynamically like this:

towerOfHanoi(n - 1, from, aux, to);

Notice the parameter positions shifted! This is because to move $n-1$ disks out of the way, you temporarily designate the Auxiliary rod as the target destination and use the To rod as the temporary helper.
